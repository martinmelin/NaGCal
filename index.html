NaGCal by martinmelin

NaGCal is a way to get a Nagios contact that automatically resolves to the person currently on call according to a schedule kept in Google Calendar.

NaGCal looks up email addresses and phone numbers from Google Contacts, so there's no extra configuration file to keep up to date.
